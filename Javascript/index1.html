World's Biggest Alzheimer Research Center

Alzheimer is a brain taking disease which mostly attacks people who are over 65, but there are some people who have been affected when they were younger because Alzheimer steals people's abilities and memories it is also known as the Brain Stealer. Alzheimer is mostly common in Western Europe and North America. Alzheimer is the most common type of Dementia. Alzheimer is a disease that robs someone's brain. Alzheimer's starts from memory loss and up to forgetting things that they do on a daily basis. For example,Walking, talking ,eating,standing, and more.Alzheimer is caused when an unusual amount of proteins create plaques and tangles around the brain. This interrupts the brain's functions.Alzheimer has a path of spreading just like any other disease. Alzheimer starts by killing the neurons and the neuron's connection. Alzheimer uses these connections to travel through and attack the remaining neurons and connections. This disease mostly attacks parts that contain the brain's memory. Like, the entorhinal cortex,hippocampus, neocortex, and the amygdala. This causes the brain's zones to get smaller. This process is called brain atrophy. Lets take Karthick's Grandma as an example. His grandma was a nice person and one day she started forgetting recent events. She would ask Karthick if he ate and than a few minutes later she would ask the same question again. This is the starting of Alzheimer, but this can also be other diseases to. Soon she started forgetting more things like, names, places, families, etc. She would ask to go home when she was at home. She thinks that she is a young lady and talks to herself in the mirror thinking it is someone else. Karthick was sad because sometimes she had forgotten his name. After a few years she started to forget how to walk, talk, or even do regualr things. She became a bed patient. Karthick was very sad after this because he loved his grandma and it was sad to see her like that everyday. This happens when Alzheimer attacks the Cerebellum. The Cerebellum is what helps humans remember their muscle memory and when Alzheimer attacks it they will forget how to walk,talk,swallow,eat ,and more.
