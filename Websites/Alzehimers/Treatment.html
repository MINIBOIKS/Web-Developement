Treatment Options

There are no Cures for Alzheimers yet (in 2022), but there are medicines to help lower the symptoms.
There are also people that can support the people who are diagnosed with this disease to live live
normally. These are some of the medicines that are given to these type of patients , Galantamine,
rivastigmine, and donepezil. There is a medicine that people take called Aducanumab that helps
destroy the amyloid plaques.
